Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known astronomical object to harbor life. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, mostly oceans, and the remaining 29% is land, consisting of continents and islands. Earth has a relatively dense atmosphere composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen, which supports a wide variety of life forms and protects the planet from harmful solar radiation.
